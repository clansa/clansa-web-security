WHAT'S INSIDE?

- Red Team Tactics — Recon, enumeration, privilege escalation

You don't hack systems by guessing. You follow a methodology. This module covers how real attackers think — passive and active recon, enumerating services without triggering alerts, and escalating from a low-privilege shell to root. No CTF shortcuts, actual technique.

- CTF Writeups — Step-by-step breakdowns of real machines

Every writeup walks through the full attack chain — what I tried, what failed, and why the working approach worked. The goal isn't to hand you flags, it's to build the mental model so you can solve the next box yourself. HackTheBox and TryHackMe machines covered.

- Network Exploitation — ARP poisoning, MITM, packet crafting

Most people learn networking to pass a certificate. This module teaches it so you can manipulate it. Covers ARP cache poisoning, intercepting traffic between two hosts, crafting raw packets with Scapy, and understanding exactly why these attacks work at the protocol level.
